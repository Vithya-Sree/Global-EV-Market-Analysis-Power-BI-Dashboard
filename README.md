# Project Title: Global EV Market Analysis Dashboard

## Objectives
The primary objective of this project is to analyze global Electric Vehicle (EV) market trends, tracking key performance metrics such as sales growth, regional adoption rates, and technological advancements to derive actionable business insights.

## Dataset Information
* **Data Source**: **International Energy Agency (IEA)**
* **Dataset**: Global EV Outlook (Data Product)
* **Citation**: This project utilizes public data tracking electric vehicle sales, historical stock, and charging infrastructure deployments provided by the **IEA Global EV Data Explorer**.

## Tools & Technologies Used
* **Power BI**: For data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard visualization.
* **Microsoft Excel**: For initial data storage and structured dataset handling.
* **GitHub**: For project documentation and version control.

## Data Cleaning and Transformation Steps
1. **Handling Missing Values**: Imputed missing data points in key numeric columns and removed records with incomplete vital fields.
2. **Data Type Formatting**: Corrected column formats for dates, currencies, and geographic attributes within Power Query.
3. **Removing Duplicates**: Filtered out redundant rows to ensure precise aggregations and KPI calculations.
4. **Column Transformations**: Split and merged categorical data fields to streamline the underlying relational model.

## Key Findings
* **Sales Growth Velocity:** Total EV sales reached **143.18M* with a Year-over-Year (YoY) growth rate of *15.56%*, indicating that overall growth momentum moderated compared to historical highs.
* **Volume Drivers:** **Passenger Cars* continue to dominate as the massive volume engine of the market, generating *89.05M* in total sales and maintaining a solid *25.91%* YoY growth rate.
* **Segment Growth Leaders:** **Trucks* showed explosive development with a *75.00%* YoY growth rate (490K units), while *Vans* also demonstrated high momentum at *41.18%* YoY growth (332K units).
* **Lagging Segments:** **2 and 3 wheelers* saw stagnant to slightly negative growth at *-0.62%* (49.94M units), and *Buses* posted a minor growth rate of *29.14%* but remain a small absolute portion of total volume (364K units).
* **Geographic Concentration:** Market volume is heavily concentrated in the Northern Hemisphere—predominantly **East Asia, **Europe, and **North America*—with minimal market footprint or expansion visible across South America, Africa, and Australia.
